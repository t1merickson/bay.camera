Bay Area Photography Webcams

Here you'll find a curated collection of webcams, web cameras, for visually tracking weather conditions, cloud and fog level and density, overall light and color, for a variety of locations through the bay area and a bit beyond.

The webcams collected here focus on the following locations on the peninsula: San Francisco, Bay Area, Golden Gate Bridge, Ocean Beach, Crissy Field, Downtown SF, Salesforce Tower, East Beach, North Beach, Bay Bridge, Transamerica Pyramid, Sunset District, Lake Merced, Embarcadero, Golden Gate Park, Presidio.

The following locations in North Bay: Mount Tam, Mount Tamalpais, Tiburon, Point Reyes, Hawk Hill, Slacker Hill, Point Bonita Lighthouse, Mill Valley, Belvedere.

The following locations in South Bay: Half Moon Bay, Pacifica, San Mateo, Redwood City, Palo Alto, Mountain View, San Jose, Cupertino, Santa Cruz, Monterey, Carmel by the Sea, Salinas.

The following locations in East Bay: Berkeley, Emeryville, Oakland, Walnut Creek, Richmond, Vallejo, Mt Diablo, Alameda.

Featuring satellite imagery from GOES-WEST, GOES-16, GOES-17, and more.
